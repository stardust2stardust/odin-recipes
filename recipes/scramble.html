Tofu Scramble

Description

An easy dish if you have a block of tofu and some veggies. The cheese is optional.

Necessary Ingredients

  • 1 block of tofu
  • Veggies suitalbe for scrambling

Optional Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Seasonings

How to Make It

  1. press excess water out of tofu
  2. break tofu into chunks
  3. cut up veggies into bite sized pieces
  4. heat up a pan and throw your veggies in to slightly soften
  5. throw in your tofu and seasonings
  6. stir around as you would like
  7. let it sit for a bit so extra water can evaporate
  8. if using cheese, sprinkle on top then cover with lid to help it melt
  9. it is ready whenever you think it looks good
