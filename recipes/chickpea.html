Chickpea Salad Sammie

Description

An easy dish if you have some chickpeas, mayo and celery or other crunchy veggie.

Necessary Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • Veggies suitalbe for crunchiness (i like celery)
  • Vegan Mayo
  • lemon juice
  • salt

How to Make It

  1. cook chickpeas from dried or use from a can
  2. drain chickpeas
  3. mash chickpeas - food processor is easiest.
  4. chop up celery or whatever crunchy veggie you like.
  5. add a scoop of mayo, a splash of lemon juice and some salt
  6. Mix up and adjust flavors how you like.
