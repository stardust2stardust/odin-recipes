Impossible Meatloaf

Description

Soooo good! No one will know it's vegan if you don't say anything.

Necessary Ingredients

  • Impossible Meat
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Ketchup
  • your favorite loaf veggies (celery, peppers, onion, etc)
  • garlic powder and onion powder
  • Worcestire Sauce

How to Make It

  1. mix everything together
  2. form into a loaf or 2 on top of parchment paper on cookie sheet
  3. bake for 35 mins at 425 degrees F
